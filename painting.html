Painting

With assembly complete, this page covers the painting process for the Portal-themed Necron Scarab. The steps are broken down so a beginner can follow along.

Supplies Required for Painting

  • Acrylic paints (base, accent, and highlight colors)
  • Assorted brushes (small detail brush, standard brush)
  • Water cup and paper towel
  • Palette or surface for mixing paints
  • Primer (spray or brush-on)

Painting Steps

  1. 1. Base Coat

    Apply an even base coat over the entire miniature after priming. This sets the main color for the Scarab.

  2. 2. Black Accents

    Add black to select areas to create contrast and define mechanical parts.

  3. 3. Gray Contrast & Highlights

    Use gray tones to build contrast and highlight raised areas, giving the model more depth.

  4. 4. Red Accents

    Add red details to tie into the Portal-themed color scheme and draw attention to key features.

  5. 5. Finished

    Review the miniature, clean up any mistakes, and add final highlights or effects as needed.
