Assembly

This page walks through the basic assembly steps for the Necron Scarab miniature. As a beginner, I'm using simple tools and straightforward techniques.

Supplies Required for Assembly

  • Sprue cutters
  • X-Acto knife
  • Sandpaper or sanding sticks
  • Plastic glue or super glue

Assembly Steps

  1. 1. Sprue Cutting

    Carefully remove the miniature pieces from the sprue using sprue cutters. Cut slightly away from the part to avoid damaging details.

  2. 2. Gluing

    Test fit each piece, then apply a small amount of glue where the parts connect. Hold the pieces together until the glue sets.

  3. 3. Fit the Pieces Together

    Once each sub-assembly is glued, fit all the pieces together to form the complete Scarab. Make final adjustments and clean up any visible seams.
